BRAIN TUMOR CLASSIFICATION

Beyond detection, the model identifies multiple brain tumor categories

Using multi-class deep learning, the system differentiates between common tumor types and healthy brain scans based on MRI features.

  • Glioma
  • Meningioma
  • Pituitary Tumor
  • Healthy Brain

Gliomas arise from glial cells and represent one of the most aggressive and common malignant brain tumors, requiring early and accurate classification.
