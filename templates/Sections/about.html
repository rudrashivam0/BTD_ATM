ABOUT THE PROJECT

NeuroScan AI is an advanced medical imaging system built on a proprietary attention-based deep learning architecture designed to analyze brain MRI scans and identify critical tumor patterns that are often difficult to detect through conventional methods.

Starting with a focus on neuro-oncology and diagnostic imaging, this system leverages modern convolutional and attention mechanisms to support a variety of clinical and research-driven applications, with the goal of improving early diagnosis and patient outcomes.
